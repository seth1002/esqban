Home page

   Abetrans Logistics LTD is one of the senior freight forwarding
   companies in Israel, it's also one of the biggest. The company has been
   active in the field of international forwarding and logistic solutions
   for 26 years at the same time kept developing and growing.


   Our customer base is wide and is getting larger all the time since we
   see you, our loyal customer, the backbone of our establishment. We are
   trying all the time to deliver the best service possible combined with
   the most competitive rate levels. We keep improving by adding the best
   technological tools along with experience manpower. All of these are
   with one purpose in mind and that is providing a second to none
   service.


   We strongly believe in personal and direct contact with our clients
   while solving any challenge in real time and providing all necessary
   information on your cargo status. It is very important to us that our
   clients remain satisfied and that each shipment we carry will be
   delivered on time and in good condition.


   Abetrans is employing 50 people in Israel, who are working in our main
   office in Tel Aviv, Haifa, Ashdod and last but not least Ben Gurion
   Airport. We also employ many people in various locations worldwide and
   that is in order to provide a fast and efficient service.


   We at Abetrans are updated at all the times with the new market trends
   and technological improvements in the field. We also strongly train are
   employees in order to provide the best customer service level.


   The most important aspect of all is that as an Abetrans client you can
   have piece of mind when you give us your cargo. It'll be dealt with
   personal care and best of abilities freeing you to deal with the rest
   of your daily chores. We will keep doing what we do the best and that
   is International Freight Forwarding.
